    About Us

    Fitness express company limited, we are one-stop fitness goods provider based in Dar es salaam,Tanzania. Registered under the company's act, cap 212 that governs the registration of companies in Tanzania. Fitness express offers retail and wholesale supply of commercial and home use equipments to individuals, companies and government institutes following all required legal procedures.

    What we do:

    We want to help you live a healthy& fit lifestyle! We do this by helping you find the most suitable equipment for your use, studio or commercial gym, keeping your budget lifestyle and fitness goals in mind. We stock a wide range of gym equipment's. strength equipments, cardio, functional and so much more. Our customer focused team is always keen to help, so please call us on 0719 617 045 to discuss your needs.

    We also perform Delivery & Installation
